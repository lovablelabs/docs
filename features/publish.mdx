Lovable makes it easy to publish your project to the web, so you can share it with friends, colleagues, or potential users. Once published, you can continue to iterate by updating your live site at any time, and even connect your own custom domain.

<Danger>
  Make sure to perform a security review before publishing your application to surface any security vulnerabilities that could expose sensitive data or lead to unauthorized access of data. Read more [here](/features/security)
</Danger>

<Note>
  Your published app will be accessible to anyone with the link. Make sure
  you're ready to share before publishing\!
</Note>
<Steps>
  <Step title="How to publish your Lovable project">
    Click the **Publish** button, in the top right corner of the project.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **Publish** in the dropdown that appears to start the deployment process. This process usually takes up to a minute. 
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Once publishing is complete, you'll receive a pop-up with the published link. Your website is now accessible by anyone, just share the link!
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Continue to iterate on your website, and when you want to publish your changes, press **Update** in the publish dropdown. 
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Connect your custom domain to give your site a polished, on-brand look by following [these steps](/features/custom-domain).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## FAQ and Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why can't I publish my site?">
    If you get an error when publishing your site, it is most likely due to an unsurfaced build error causing the current version of your website not to function. Try troubleshooting the issue by asking the AI to investigate it. 
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why can't I see my changes on the published version of my site?">
    To publish new changes to your live site you have to press **Update** in the publish dropdown. They don't get automatically pushed to your published project. 
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I change my published link?">
    To change your published link you have to rename your project. Go to the **Project settings** > **Rename**. For the renaming to update the link, make sure to press **Update** in the publish dropdown after the change. 
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I unpublish my project?">
    Yes! You can do so in your Project Settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
