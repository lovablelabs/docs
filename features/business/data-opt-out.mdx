title: "Data opt-out"
description: "Enhanced data privacy controls for your organization"
icon: "server"
---

Training data exclusion is a feature available for **Business plan and above**, ensuring that your proprietary code, projects, and data are never used to train AI models or perform evaluations. This protects your sensitive information and reinforces your organization’s data privacy.

Workspace admins and owners can enable **Data collection opt out** in **Settings → Privacy & Security**.
