OIDC (OpenID Connect) is the recommended protocol for SSO when your identity provider supports it. Configure SSO in **Settings → Workspace → Identity**. SSO is available on **Business** and **Enterprise** plans.

### Choose your OIDC provider

### Quick reference

- Redirect URI: https://auth.lovable.dev/\_\_/auth/handler
- Scopes: `openid`, `email`
